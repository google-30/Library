MEMBERSHIP :


Fill a form that will require you to bring two recent photographs. A serial number that marks your turn in the membership process will be assigned to you on the day you submit the form. From time to time, you will need to stay informed whether your library card has been issued or not. Two library cards are issued, one for the Central Library and another for the Book bank. When your turn arrives, take your ID card along with you as it will be assigned a bar code. You are then allowed to issue books.


At a time, one is allowed to issue 2 books for a period of 15 days; thereafter a fine of Re 1 per day is charged.

Book Bank :


USIT runs a book bank that supports the financially weak students and those belonging to the SC/ST category to whom books are lent for the whole academic semester. After a duration of 2 weeks from the start of the semester, the book bank is opened for the general category students as well.


All you have to do is to submit a written application specifying your name, roll number and the books you need at the counter. The serial number assigned implies your status in the waiting list. You are free to avail the books when your turn comes.


The collection of books is categorized in five sections:


* Book bank cum text book section


* General section


* Reference section


* Periodical section


* Audio Visual cum Digital Library section


USIT is also a member of DELNET and OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) on various libraries in the country that are accessible through Web access.
